Practice Task Selection for XLogo-Mini

We have created 20-30 practice tasks for each exercise in XLogo-Mini. We would like to invite you to help us choose the best tasks for each exercise. Your expertise in this matter would be greatly valued. Please follow the steps outlined below to select the tasks.

Step 1: Open the link: https://xlogo.inf.ethz.ch/test/practice/#/mini

Once you open the link, click on either "Practice More" or "Mehr Ã¼ben?" to access all the practice tasks for each exercise. Review the tasks and proceed to step 2 to share your feedback with us via google sheet.

Step 2: Open the google sheet and finish the survey questions.

You are provided with a Google Sheet containing survey questions that need to be answered for each practice task. Each row in the sheet corresponds to a specific practice task, and there are 4 questions for each. Please select the appropriate answer for each question.

Here are the questions:

Q1: Rate the overall quality of the synthesized task.

  • Poor quality (Task cannot be solved or is very confusing, a teacher won't present this task to students)
  • Satisfactory quality (A teacher would present this task to students with some modifications)
  • Excellent quality (A teacher can present this task to students without any modifications)

Q2: (Optional) How does the difficulty of this task compare to the reference task? (If you think the labeled difficulty is incorrect, please choose the correct difficulty level. Otherwise, no need to make a selection.)

  • Easy (A student can solve this practice if he can solve the reference exercise)
  • Medium (A student should be able to solve this task, but he needs to perform additional steps to solution it)
  • Difficult (Might be challenging, but a student might still be able to solve it with some effort or additional guidance)

Q3: Choose 1-3 tasks that you like the most.

  • Please select 1-3 tasks for each group of practice tasks.

Q4: (Optional) Any additional comments? (If you think the overall quality is poor or the labeled difficulty is incorrect, please provide your comments.)

Guidelines

1. It would be convenient to keep two pages open side-by-side: the webpage which describes each task and the google sheet to enter the rating for the practice.
